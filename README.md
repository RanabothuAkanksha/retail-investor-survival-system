# Retail Investor Survival System

## Overview

Retail Investor Survival System is a behavioral finance analytics platform designed to identify investing mistakes and quantify their impact on long-term wealth creation.

Instead of focusing on stock picking, the platform analyzes investor behavior.

The goal is to answer:

- Why do retail investors underperform?
- How much does concentration risk hurt returns?
- How much wealth is lost through emotional trading?
- How diversified is a portfolio?

---

## Key Metrics

### Behavior Score

Measures investor discipline.

### Diversification Score

Measures portfolio concentration.

### Emotional Trading Index

Measures panic selling and FOMO buying.

### Risk Score

Measures portfolio volatility and concentration.

### Wealth Projection

Compares current behavior versus disciplined investing strategies.
